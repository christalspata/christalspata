About

A fundamentally different kind
of real estate practice.

The traditional real estate model asks agents to be experts in a neighborhood and serve as many clients as possible. I built the opposite.

I become an expert in each client — their financial structure, family dynamics, tax exposure, security requirements, timeline, and long-term vision — and follow them across state lines. When a client acquires a lakefront compound in one state, relocates their primary residence to another, and sells a property in a third, they work with one advisor who understands the full picture. Not three strangers who each know one market.

I call this the reverse agent model. Instead of knowing every house on the block, I know every detail about the people I represent. That depth is what allows me to coordinate with wealth advisors, estate attorneys, tax strategists, and family offices at the level these transactions demand.

My practice is augmented by proprietary AI intelligence systems that monitor markets, track ownership changes, build prospect research dossiers, and synthesize data around the clock. Technology handles the breadth. I handle the depth, the judgment, and the relationships.
