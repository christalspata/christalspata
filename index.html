The Challenge

Multi-State Transactions Are Uniquely Complex

Juggling Agents Across States

Different agents in different markets with different standards, timelines, and loyalties. No single point of coordination means details fall through the cracks.

Tax Surprises That Cost Millions

Exit taxes, residency audits, mansion taxes, and estate tax exposure. The wrong timing or structure can cost you 5-10x what proper planning would have saved.

Selling in one state while buying in another creates cascading timing risks. Bridge financing, 1031 exchange windows, and closing coordination require precision.
